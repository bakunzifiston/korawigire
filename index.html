Who we are

A multi-service house built around skill, not just trade

Founded in Rubavu District, Korawigire Multi Services & Money Radio Ltd brings creative services, vocational training, and Money Radio advertising together under one roof — turning ideas into tangible value for businesses and individuals across Rwanda.

1

Our mission

To deliver professional radio advertising, custom tailoring, graphic design, printing, and carpentry solutions while expanding digital access, creativity, and skills for youth and entrepreneurs.

2

Our vision

To become Rubavu's leading multi-service hub, combining creativity, media, and technology to uplift communities and grow sustainable businesses.
